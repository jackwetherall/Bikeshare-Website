Affordable & Sustainable

Quickly and ethically travel around your city with the UK's first electric bike share. We are ideal for quick journeys. Click the dowload button bellow to install our app & start using our services.

Getting Started

Open our app to be directed to the nearest bike, and scan the QR code at the top of the handle-bars to begin.

Payment

Scan the bikes QR code to be taken to our payment page & start the session.

Return

To return, simply open our app and cycle to the nearest drop-off point. Follow the apps instructions to end the session.

Memberships & Pricing

Standard

free

Rates are £0.08 per minuet and £5.00 for 24hours for users without subscription. Our subscriptions give regular users monthly benefits.

Silver

Users can reserve bikes, recieve 2 24hour unlocks per month & 30 free riding minuets per week which can be used over multiple sessions.

Gold

£29.99/pm
